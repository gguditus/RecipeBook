London Broil

Cooking one or two roasts is easier, cheaper, more efficient, and sometimes just as delicious as cooking steaks for a large party!
This is a recipe for one such roast with a sauce that is to die for. The cut of beef is cheap and easy to find. And to top it all off, it eats well in any season of the year.

Pasta Dough

This is my favorite recipe to make Fettuccine (or any other pasta that is rolled out thinly). It is not all that much work, but it is as impressive as it is delicious. I make it for special occasions, to impress people, or to just have a darn good meal. This recipe is adapted from Helen Rennie's recipe.
